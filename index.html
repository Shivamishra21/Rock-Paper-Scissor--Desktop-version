This is a hand game which will be played between you and computer.
Rock paper scissors is a hand game usually played between two people (you and computer in this case), in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes(Rock, paper or scissor) with an outstretched hand. These shapes are "Rock" (a closed fist), "paper" (a flat hand), and "scissors" (a fist with the index finger and middle finger extended, forming a V). "Scissors" is identical to the two-fingered V sign (also indicating "victory" or "peace") except that it is pointed horizontally instead of being held upright in the air. A simultaneous, zero-sum game, it has only two possible outcomes: a draw, or a win for one player and a loss for the other.

How to play

  • You and your opponent(computer) will each form one of the three objects—either rock, paper or scissors.
  • Then name of the winner will be based on which object they played. Every game will be different, so stay alert!
  • Both players must throw at the same time. If one player delays, the result isn’t trustworthy, and you should start the game over.
  • Don’t play the same object every time. Switch it up to fool your opponent. Determine the winner.
  • After you’ve both played an object, look to see who’s won. Each object wins against one shape and loses to another.
    • Rock “crushes” scissors
    • Paper “covers” rock
    • But paper is “cut” by scissors
    • Scissors is “crushed” by rock but “cuts” paper
  • who picks the stronger of the two objects is the winner.
  • If both players throw the same object, it’s a tie. In this situation, simply play again until there is a clear winner.
